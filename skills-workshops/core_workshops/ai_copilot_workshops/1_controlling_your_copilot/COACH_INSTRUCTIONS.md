# Coach Instructions

How to run this workshop.

## Schedule

- Intro
- Part One: Understanding
  - Exercise
  - Mini plenary
- Part Two: Refactoring
  - Exercise
  - Mini plenary
- Part Three: Extending
  - Exercise
  - Mini plenary
- Wrap up

## Intro

During the intro you...

- Describe Ask, Edit and Agent modes
- Demonstrate Copilot going a bit wild in Agent mode. You could do this by giving it a high level instruction like "turn this app into a web app".
- Explain that Copilot can be reined in / controlled in several ways
- Demonstrate
  - **Copilot instructions**, for coding guidelines and general behaviour
  - **Custom prompts**, for more specific use cases
  - **Custom chatmodes**, for high level tasks where you need more specific control than Ask, Edit or Agent provide.
- Explain the structure of the workshop and the first task
- Explain the learning objectives
- Re-iterate the need for good git practice here!

> You can explain the tasks 2 and 3, as needed, at the end of the preceding plenary

## Mini Plenaries

- Ask everyone to DM you on Zoom with a number out of 10 according to how they got on with the task
- Use these numbers to decide who is going to share their work with the cohort
- After the first, try to find someone who has taken a different approach / had a different experience for the second one

> In the first mini-plenary, it would be useful to get learners to share a number out of 10 twice – once at the start and once towards the end – so you can see whether their understanding improved during the plenary.

## To Pair or Not to Pair?

You can choose whether or not to pair learners up.

## Wrap Up

Ask learners to DM you with a number out of 10 for each learning objective where 10 means they completely achieved it.
