# Debugging

## Introduction

Debugging is not just about fixing a mistake; it is about becoming a detective. This workshop is designed to help you slow down, stop "guessing" at fixes, and develop the patience required to discover exactly where a problem lies before you touch the code.

Bugs are an inevitable part of coding. Even the most experienced engineers spend a significant portion of their day debugging. Today, we want to normalise that process and give you a toolkit to handle it with confidence.

## How to Engage with This Task

This session is interactive and investigative. Your coach will first introduce the session and demonstrate the approach we expect you to take when debugging. You will then have 20-30 minutes to do some debugging. At the end, the cohort will regroup, do some debugging together and reflect on the process.
